Executive Summary

The CG Portal Feedback System represents a significant step forward in the Government of Chhattisgarh's digital transformation journey.

This comprehensive web-based platform has been specifically designed to modernize and streamline the process of collecting, managing, and analyzing citizen feedback across multiple government departments for internal government use. The system provides government employees with efficient tools to record, track, and manage citizen feedback received through various channels such as phone calls, in-person visits, and other communication methods. This platform bridges the gap between citizens and government departments by ensuring that government staff can efficiently process and respond to every concern raised by the public.

The platform serves as a central hub for internal feedback management, enabling government officials and staff to make data-driven decisions and continuously improve public services. By implementing this system, the Government of Chhattisgarh demonstrates its commitment to transparency, accountability, and citizen-centric governance through efficient internal processes.

Project Vision and Objectives

Vision Statement

To create an efficient, transparent, and accessible internal digital platform that empowers government employees to effectively collect, manage, and respond to citizen feedback, enabling departments to deliver better public services through streamlined internal processes.

Primary Objectives

  • Internal Process Enhancement: Provide government employees with efficient tools to record and manage citizen feedback received through various channels
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamline the feedback collection and management process for government staff and officials
  • Data-Driven Governance: Enable evidence-based decision making through comprehensive feedback analytics for internal use
  • Internal Transparency: Ensure clear communication and tracking channels between different government departments and levels
  • Service Quality Improvement: Use systematically collected citizen feedback to continuously enhance public service delivery

Key Features and Capabilities

Citizen Feedback Collection

The system provides government employees with tools to efficiently record citizen feedback from multiple channels:

  • Call Center Integration: Streamlined interface for recording feedback received through government helplines and phone calls
  • In-Person Feedback Recording: Tools for government staff to document feedback received during citizen visits to offices
  • Multi-Department Support: Feedback can be categorized and routed to appropriate departments (Health, Finance, Tax, etc.)
  • Flexible Feedback Types: Support for various types of feedback including satisfaction ratings, complaints, suggestions, and service requests

Advanced Administrative Dashboard

The administrative interface provides comprehensive tools for managing the entire feedback ecosystem:

  • Real-time Statistics: Live dashboard showing feedback volumes, satisfaction rates, and department performance
  • Status Management: Tools to track and update the status of feedback from submission to resolution
  • Search and Filter: Advanced search capabilities to quickly locate specific feedback entries
  • User Management: Complete system for managing user accounts and access permissions
  • Department Administration: Tools for adding, editing, and managing government departments

Professional Reporting System

One of the standout features of the system is its comprehensive reporting capabilities:

  • PDF Export Functionality: Generate professional PDF reports with government branding
  • Department-wise Reports: Create specific reports for individual departments
  • Comprehensive Analytics: Detailed statistics including satisfaction rates, resolution times, and trend analysis
  • Executive Summary Reports: High-level overview reports for senior management
  • Scheduled Reporting: Automated generation of periodic reports

Multi-language Support

Recognizing the linguistic diversity of Chhattisgarh and the need for government employees to work efficiently, the system includes:

  • Hindi Language Support: Complete interface available in Hindi for local government staff
  • English Interface: Professional English interface for official documentation and reporting
  • Dynamic Language Switching: Government employees can switch between languages seamlessly based on their preference
  • Localized Content: All system messages and labels available in both languages for better usability
